About

I am a researcher and undergraduate student at Arizona State University, studying Electrical Engineering and Computational Mathematical Sciences, and a member of Barrett, the Honors College.

My dream is a career in research at the intersection of signal processing and data analysis, and I plan to pursue graduate studies in Computer Science to this end.

Right now, I lead the executive board of Sun Devil Robotics and am a member of the Luminsoity Lab, an ASU strategic initiative.

I have performed grant-funded research in the Nanoelectronics and Integration Lab at ASU under the direction of Dr. Hongbin Yu and did a research exchange with the Social Robotics Lab at the National University of Singapore.

Hopefully I can populate this website with the fun things that I make!
